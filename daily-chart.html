How We Calculate the Bite %

Every hour starts with a baseline probability. As environmental conditions align to trigger feeding behavior, the percentage increases up to a maximum of 100%.

Base Probability: 10%
Falling Barometric Pressure: +35%
Full or New Moon: +20%
Light Rain (0.1 - 0.5 in): +20%
Ideal Temperature (55°F - 75°F): +15%
